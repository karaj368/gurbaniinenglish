About Gůrǝbȧņē Transliteration

The Gůrǝbȧņē transliteration system is a scientific approach to representing Gurmukhi script in Roman letters. This system preserves the exact pronunciation and phonetic nuances of the original Punjabi text, making it accessible to scholars and devotees worldwide while maintaining linguistic accuracy.
